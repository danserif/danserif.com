[About]

Dan Newman. Dog Dad. Creative Director. List Maker.

(^_^)/~   I’m Dan, a designer who’s been building brands, teams, and online/offline things since the early 2000s.

Over the past two decades I’ve led design teams and worked hands-on at startups like Axiom, Stedi, and Xero; as well as at agencies, designing award-winning work for the likes of Klim and Te Papa Tongarewa (Museum of New Zealand).

Making authentic and aesthetically unique work with talented colleagues/friends is one of my favourite things.
Bonus points for [mono] fonts, glyphs, pixels, patterns, puns, and alliteration.

I have a Master’s degree in Computer Graphic Design from the Whanganui School of Design, which included a scholarship to study at Nottingham University (England), and FH Oberösterreich (Austria).

These days, I live in Te Whanganui-a-Tara (Wellington), Aotearoa (New Zealand) with my partner, Noush, and our dog, Ada, a black/chocolate labradoodle who loves sticks, fetch, and the beach. Together we run Knolling, a family office and design studio focused on making our own products.

I love fonts, ASCII, the Internet, lists, black/white photography, watching cricket, and taking the dog to the beach.

I’m currently building [REDACTED] and advising/designing for a small number of startups.
